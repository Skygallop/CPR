How can you get to know us and our mission?


At the beginning of our campaign, as the project was still in its infancy, we started to begin growing our social media in the hopes that people would watch our content and support our cause. If you were around at the time, you might remember seeing posters stuck to every telephone pole the eye could see. We wanted the people to see our proposal before any plans on the terrains in the area were made.

Thanks to our efforts, different communities across the city began to give us their full support, and in some time, various cities began to invite us over for talks and to discuss plans to expand the project.

Community support has been the backbone of our organization.
As with the power of the people and their unity, their will to fight to make their city more attractive, more inclusive, more developed, more united than ever, we were able to get the ball rolling and begin to develop the project.
