Californias Project for Revitalization














Bringing communities back to life.


The Project for Revitalization aims to help the Californias in as many aspects as possible. As everyone might be able to identify, one of the main problems across the entire state is visual pollution, especially with abandoned, barren lots with a lot of potential. Even though we do have beautiful spaces, most of our cities are clearly polluted, and it's quite notorious to see how little of a priority it has been given by the government and our community. This is why our main goal is to transform them into not just pleasing to look at spaces, but ones with a new life, a brand new assigned purpose, which all of us can take advantage of. With this project, we are also trying to push forward more ways in which we can help our community by opening areas for public enjoyment, business opportunities and charitable work.


Spaces, assigned to serve the common good, and be a benchmark for progress.

As stated before, the CPR greenspaces will have a much bigger purpose than your average park, as we intend to help local businesses by providing them with an attractive and welcoming environment around their location. We, as an organization, want to foment young entrepreneurs and give them the opportunity they deserve to test their new ideas. As we grant them the opportunity to shine, we also think of those who never were granted an opportunity and fell into despair. So that's why we intend to use the profits for those in need, as our intentions are still with the community. That's why we don't take any profits, what we do with the surplus is to help fund and open soup kitchens and food banks across the state.

And lastly, our final milestone is for our greenspaces to unite the people and create a sense of a welcoming community. We believe that the sentiment of inclusion is strengthened greatly by the implementation of physical activities. And how do we plan on making this possible?, by implementing recreational spaces, such as sports courts, in which children and teenagers can play in. Our greenspaces will help to better healthy habits and activities, by providing a clean and safe environment for everyone. The CPR is also planning on helping to promote sports-related social activities, like weekly sport coaching sessions, marathons, and cycling events.
